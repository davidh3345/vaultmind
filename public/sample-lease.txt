RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (SAMPLE)

1. Parties. This lease is between the Landlord, Aurora Properties LLC, and the Tenant.

2. Rent. The Tenant shall pay rent of $2,400 per month, due on the first day of each
calendar month. A late fee of $75 applies after the fifth day.

3. Security Deposit. The security deposit is two months' rent ($4,800). It is refundable
within 30 days of move-out, less any documented damages beyond normal wear and tear.

4. Term and Termination. The initial term is twelve months. Either party may terminate
at the end of the term by giving ninety (90) days written notice. Early termination by the
Tenant requires payment of two months' rent as an early-termination fee.

5. Pets. Pets are not permitted without prior written consent and a $500 pet deposit.

6. Maintenance. The Landlord is responsible for structural repairs and the HVAC system.
The Tenant is responsible for routine upkeep and any damage they cause.

7. Subletting. Subletting is prohibited without the Landlord's written approval.

8. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California.
